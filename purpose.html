What is the problem?

Email is a widely used tool for both business and personal communication, but people often encounter unwanted spam messages. Spam typically refers to messages filled with unwanted content such as advertisements or scam attempts. Spam can clutter users' email inboxes, causing them to overlook genuine messages or enticing users to click on deceptive links, posing a security risk. Therefore, effective spam filtering and email management are important to protect users from unwanted content and ensure easy access to genuine messages.

Our Purpose

The purpose of the spam shield application, which offers the user 3 different modes (daily, business and commercial) in addition to the general mode for spam scanning, is to protect you from all unwanted e-mails.
