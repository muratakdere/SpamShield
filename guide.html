How do we use Spam Shield?

Main Menu:

The main menu page of our application features the mode section for email spam filtering. Clicking on the "General Mode" directs you to the filtering page in general mode. When you press the "Other Modes" button, our application offers you the opportunity to search in three different modes.

General Mode:

On this page, when you enter the email you're curious about into the designated field and click the "Scan" button, you'll be able to see in the results whether your email is spam or not.

Other Modes:

When you click on the "Other Modes" button on the main menu page, the application directs you to the "Select Mode" page. Spam Shield presents you with three different modes: Daily, Business, and Commercial modes. These modes offer more specific filtering options, allowing you to better organize your emails.

Sample Mode:

You received an email offering you the opportunity to redeem your accumulated points for a discount from a famous brand where you've shopped before. However, you're unsure whether this email is spam or legitimate. You can switch to the commercial mode to check it out.

White and Blacklist:

These two lists are created to assist our model in working more efficiently. The Blacklist is a list where you can add words you think are spam, while the Whitelist is where you add words you believe are not spam.
